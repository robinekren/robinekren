Personal journey

Career

Robin Ekren discovered his passion for online marketing when he began using Instagram marketing. With a vision to share his knowledge and build a community, Robin started creating content that quickly helped him gain thousands of followers. What started as a hobby soon became a professional business.

After years of refining and experimenting, Robin realized that Instagram was not just a platform for personal success, but a valuable business opportunity. In 2025, Robin launched his first online courses, teaching others how to use Instagram for marketing purposes. These courses grew rapidly, propelling him to a successful career as an Instagram expert.

But Robin wanted more: he wanted to create a system that would help other entrepreneurs succeed as well. He founded Eko University, a platform that not only shares knowledge but empowers participants to scale it and make it profitable in the long run.

Today, Robin's Goal is to help thousands of people build successful businesses through Eko University, and this is just the beginning. With his platform, Robin aims to create a lasting impact on entrepreneurs worldwide.
